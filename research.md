# Research

My research applies **advanced econometric methods and causal machine learning** to address critical questions in development economics, environmental sustainability, and digital transformation.

---

## Current Research Projects

### 1. ESG Performance in Emerging Economies
Using **Double Machine Learning (DML)** and causal inference frameworks to analyze firm-level Environmental, Social, and Governance outcomes in developing countries.  
*Collaborators: V. Csapi | Status: Active*

### 2. Energy-Globalization-Sustainability Nexus
Applying **DoWhy** causal analysis and **DirectLiNGAM** for structural discovery to examine the relationship between energy consumption, globalization, and environmental sustainability in transition economies.  
*Collaborators: L.T. Abdisa, H.A. Legas, A. Shumetie | Status: Active*

### 3. Identity Economics & ESG Decision Making
Investigating how social identity and institutional trust shape ESG-related decisions and outcomes.  
*Status: Working paper*

### 4. Food Price Shocks and Child Nutrition
Investigating the differential impacts of food price changes on child nutritional status with a gender lens, using Ethiopian household survey data.  
*Collaborators: A. Mehare, L.T. Abdisa | Status: Published*
